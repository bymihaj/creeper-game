Fluid Defense

Energy: 50
How to play:
1. The purple fluid (Creeper) spreads from emitters. If it touches your Base (green), you lose.
2. Build Collectors (yellow) to generate energy. They must be built near your existing network (green lines).
3. Build Turrets (red) to destroy the fluid and the emitters. Turrets require energy to fire.
4. Goal: Destroy all purple Emitters with your turrets to win.
